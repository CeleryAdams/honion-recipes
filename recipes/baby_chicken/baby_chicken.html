Baby Chicken

this is a delicious baby chicken

So this is a very cute and delicious snack. Maybe it is too tiny and furry to eat. But if you wait a few months you can eat it. Trust me. It's very tasty. You can boil or fry the baby chicken. You can even make a salad. Wait until it is no longer as cute as this. When the tastiness is more than the cuteness, you can eat this baby chicken without any regret. You will know when the time comes. Also you need to be hungry.

The recipe is for one baby chicken. Let's say we make some soup.

what you need to cook baby chicken

  • a little chickaboo
  • if you don't have a chickaboo you can start with an egg
  • some seeds
  • some grass
  • wait a while
  • pot of water with seasonings

how to cook the chicken

  1. grow the chicken
  2. put the chicken upside down and make it go sleepy
  3. put the baby chicken in the pot with seasonings
  4. enjoy!
